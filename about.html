Our Purpose

A Mission Rooted
in the Love of Reading

MYTHOS was born from a simple belief — that every person deserves a great story. We set out to build a platform that makes discovering, reading, and sharing books as easy and joyful as possible.

Whether you are a child finding magic in your first picture book, a student diving into study notes, or an adult revisiting classic literature, MYTHOS meets you exactly where you are. We curate, we recommend, and we celebrate every reader's journey.

Our mission is not just to sell books — it is to nurture a lifelong love of reading and to make storytelling accessible to everyone across India and beyond.

Looking Ahead

Building India's Most
Beloved Reading Community

We envision a future where a child in a small town has the same access to quality books as one in a metro city. Where authors from every corner of the country can share their stories with the world. Where reading is not a privilege but a way of life.

MYTHOS is building that future — one book, one reader, one story at a time. Our platform will continue to grow its catalogue, invest in regional language content, and support emerging writers through the MYTHOS Creator Program.

The Journey

Our Story So Far

2023
The Idea Sparks

Kavya and Karthik, two avid readers, noticed how hard it was to find curated, age-appropriate books online in India. The idea for MYTHOS was born over a shared cup of chai and a towering stack of novels.

2023
2024
2024
Platform Launches

MYTHOS officially launched with 500+ books across children's, teen, and adult genres. Within the first month, over 1,000 readers signed up and the community began to take shape.

2025
Creator Program Begins

We opened the MYTHOS Creator Program, inviting independent authors and storytellers to publish directly on our platform. Dozens of original works found their first readers through MYTHOS.

2025
2026
2026
Growing & Expanding

With 5,000+ books and 12,000+ readers, MYTHOS is expanding into regional languages and launching Study Notes — helping students access curated academic content alongside their favourite reads.
